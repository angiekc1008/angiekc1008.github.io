Another brand I developed and created a mock stationery set for. This is a non-profit organization that aids in the goal of reforesting the Earth. As deforestation continues to happen, Pine Shield fights to create reserves where tree chopping is prohibited, spread awareness on the issue, and deliver seeds to every donator so they can directly contribute to the cause. Made in ART 235 with Clark Most. Adobe Illustrator and InDesign.
